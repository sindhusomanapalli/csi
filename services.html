Services

At Curve Space Interiors, each project begins with a deep understanding of the client’s lifestyle needs and vision. We plan every detail layouts, materials, colors, lighting and furniture to create a space that blends elegance and functionality. Whether it’s an apartment, villa, commercial office or retail interior our team ensures smooth execution with premium craftsmanship and high-quality finishes.

Residential Interiors

  • Designs for apartments, villas, independent houses, and studio homes.
  • Space planning that improves comfort, function, and aesthetic appeal.
  • Selection of colors, lighting, materials, décor, and furniture.
  • Complete home makeovers from concept to execution.
  • Customized wardrobes, TV units, false ceilings, and décor elements.

Commercial Interiors

  • Interiors for offices, retail stores, showrooms, cafés, and workspaces.
  • Professional lighting, furniture, and material selection.
  • Glass partitions, workstations, and reception design solutions.
  • Studios and work areas planned with ergonomic and aesthetic balance
  • Co-working spaces created for collaboration and creativity

Modular Kitchens

  • Customized modular cabinets tailored to your cooking style.
  • Smart storage options to maximize space and organize essentials.
  • High-quality countertops, backsplashes, and fixtures.
  • Appliance integration for a seamless, modern kitchen look.
  • Layouts designed for easy movement and efficiency.

Custom Furniture & Woodwork

  • Sofas, beds, wardrobes, dining tables, TV units, and study desks.
  • Choice of premium materials, finishes, and hardware.
  • Designs that combine comfort, durability, and style.
  • Built-in storage options for clutter-free living.
  • Tailor-made furniture designed to fit your space perfectly.

False Ceiling & Lighting Design

  • Modern and decorative false ceiling concepts.
  • Ambient, task, and accent lighting for the right mood.
  • LED, cove lighting, spotlights, and chandelier placement.
  • Energy-efficient lighting solutions.
  • Enhances height, ambience, and overall visual appeal.

Renovation & Remodeling

  • Upgrading old interiors to modern, stylish spaces.
  • Structural rework and layout optimization.
  • New flooring, wall finishes, and paint.
  • Electrical, plumbing, and storage improvements.
  • Complete transformation while retaining structural safety.

Space Planning & 3D Visualization

  • Professional layout planning to utilize every inch of space.
  • 3D renders to show the final look before work begins.
  • Helps clients make design and material decisions easily.
  • Ensures designs are accurate and aligned with expectations.
  • Prevents mistakes and supports smooth execution.

Turnkey Interior Solutions

  • End-to-end interior service from design to final handover.
  • We manage materials, labor, schedules, and supervision.

  • Ensures timely, quality delivery of the project.
  • Ideal for busy homeowners and commercial clients.
